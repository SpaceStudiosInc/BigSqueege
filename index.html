Meet the Founder

Experience That Counts

Since 1993, Mike Nichols has dedicated his career to perfecting window and exterior cleaning across Arkansas and Oklahoma. With over 30 years of hands-on experience, Mike has mastered industry best practices and innovative cleaning techniques that ensure exceptional results.

Mike’s expertise spans residential homes, commercial properties, and storefronts, allowing Squeege Solutions to provide tailored solutions that meet the unique needs of each client. Licensed, insured, and deeply committed to delivering outstanding service, Mike personally oversees quality assurance on every project.

Under Mike’s leadership, Squeege Solutions have grown from a small local business into a trusted regional brand recognized for professionalism, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Mike Nichols — Owner and Founder of Squeege Solutions
