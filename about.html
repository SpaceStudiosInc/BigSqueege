Meet the Founder

Experience That Matters

Since founding The Window Guys in 1993, Mike Nichols has cultivated over 30 years of expertise in window and exterior cleaning. His mastery of advanced techniques and commitment to excellence ensure consistently outstanding results.

Mike’s experience spans residential, commercial, and storefront projects, enabling tailored solutions that meet each client’s unique needs. Fully licensed and insured, he personally oversees every project to maintain uncompromising quality.

Under Mike’s guidance, The Window Guys have grown into a respected regional leader known for professionalism, reliability, and exceptional customer service.
