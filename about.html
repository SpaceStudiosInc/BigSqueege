Meet the Founder

Experience That Counts

Since founding Squeege Solutions in 1993, Mike Nichols has built a distinguished career in window and exterior cleaning services, serving Arkansas and Oklahoma with unparalleled expertise. With over three decades of experience, he has honed advanced techniques and industry-leading practices to deliver consistently exceptional results.

Mr. Nichols’ proficiency encompasses residential, commercial, and storefront properties, allowing Squeege Solutions to provide customized solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs. As a fully licensed and insured professional, he ensures uncompromising quality by personally overseeing every project, maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship.

Under his leadership, Squeege Solutions have evolved from a local operation into a respected regional enterprise, renowned for professionalism, reliability, and outstanding customer service.

Mike Nichols — Owner and Founder
