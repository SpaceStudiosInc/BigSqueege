What We Offer

We specialize in providing expert consulting services tailored for window cleaning businesses, helping you optimize operations and increase client satisfaction. Our offerings include:

  • Residential Cleaning Consulting: Best practices, techniques, and efficient workflows for homes.
  • Commercial Property Consulting: Streamlining large-scale cleaning operations for office buildings and complexes.
  • Storefront Window Cleaning Consulting: Tailored solutions for retail businesses to maintain clear, welcoming displays.
  • Business Growth Strategies: Marketing, customer retention, and operational improvements.
  • Quality Assurance Guidance: Training and standards to ensure exceptional results on every job.

Who We Are

The Window Guys are a team of experienced professionals passionate about elevating the standards of the window cleaning industry. Founded by Mike Nichols, we combine decades of practical expertise with innovative consulting solutions designed to empower businesses throughout Arkansas and Oklahoma.

Our approach is client-focused and results-driven, ensuring that every consulting engagement delivers measurable improvements in service quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Licensed and insured, we uphold the highest ethical and professional standards, committed to fostering growth and long-term success for your business.

Meet the Founder

Experience That Counts

Since founding The Window Guys in 1993, Mike Nichols has built a distinguished career in window and exterior cleaning services, serving Arkansas and Oklahoma with unparalleled expertise. With over three decades of experience, he has honed advanced techniques and industry-leading practices to deliver consistently exceptional results.

Mr. Nichols’ proficiency encompasses residential, commercial, and storefront properties, allowing The Window Guys to provide customized solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs. As a fully licensed and insured professional, he ensures uncompromising quality by personally overseeing every project, maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship.

Under his leadership, The Window Guys have evolved from a local operation into a respected regional enterprise, renowned for professionalism, reliability, and outstanding customer service.
